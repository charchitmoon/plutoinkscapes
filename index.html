Vision
Charchit

Charchit is an independent artist and creative thinker exploring the intersection of energy, environment, and imagination. Through PlutoInkScapes, he translates complex global challenges into visual stories that anyone can feel — not just understand.

Based in India. Open to collaborations worldwide.

The Problem

The cost of inaction.

We are borrowing time from the future. The energy crisis isn't a distant threat — it's the reality of our present. This is the cost of progress built on extraction.

These staggering realities moved me to create PlutoInkScapes. Art has the power to reframe our perspective and imagine a world where we work with nature, not against it.

"We cannot build a future on the fumes of the past. The transition is not a choice, it is an imperative."

Clean Energy

Clean Energy Solutions

The technology exists. The transition is inevitable. We just need the collective will to accelerate it.

☀️

Solar Power

Harnessing the infinite energy of our star. Reliable, clean, and increasingly accessible to power the future.

  • A solar panel installed today will still produce clean energy in 2049
  • Solar is now the cheapest electricity in history
  • India's solar capacity grew 300% in the last decade
🌬️

Wind Energy

Capturing the breath of the earth. A single modern offshore turbine can power thousands of homes.

  • The world's largest turbine powers 16,000 homes
  • Wind costs have fallen 70% since 2010
  • No greenhouse gas emissions during operation
💧

Hydroelectric

The ancient power of water. Reliable, renewable, and responsible for the largest share of clean energy today.

  • Hydropower provides 17% of global electricity
  • Run-of-river plants have minimal environmental impact
  • Pumped hydro is the world's largest energy storage
